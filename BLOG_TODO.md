- Small business owners in RI (especially Cranston, Providence, Warwick)
- 10-50 employees, $500K-$5M revenue
- Non-technical but growth-minded

### Content Goals

1. **SEO**: Target "Rhode Island + software/web development" keywords
2. **Lead Generation**: Each post should have clear CTA
3. **Trust Building**: Demonstrate local expertise and results

### Publishing Schedule

- **Target**: 2 posts per month minimum

- **Cross-promotion**: Share on LinkedIn, local business groups

- Organic traffic growth to blog

- Time on page and engagement rates
