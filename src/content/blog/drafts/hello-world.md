This is the beginning of something I've been meaning to do for a while: write about the things I think about while building software.

## Why write?

Most of what I've learned over the past decade hasn't come from documentation — it's come from solving problems in systems that don't behave the way you'd expect. I want to start capturing some of that here.

## What to expect

I'll write about backend systems, workflow design, developer tools, and the occasional opinion on how we build things. Nothing on a schedule — just when something feels worth sharing.

If any of that sounds interesting, stick around.
